Privacy Policy


At Valentine Finder, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we receive and collect when you use our website, as well as how we use, share, and protect that information.


Information We Collect


When you use Valentine Finder, we may collect personal information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, and preferences for potential dates. We use this information to match you with potential dates based on your preferences.


How We Use Your Information


We use the personal information you provide to match you with potential dates. We do not share your personal information with anyone without your consent. Your necessary information will only be shared with your potential date, and we will not use it for any other purpose without your permission.


Data Security


We take precautions to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We use secure servers and encryption to safeguard your information.


Your Choices


You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. You can also choose whether or not to participate in our matchmaking service.


Changes to this Policy


We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
